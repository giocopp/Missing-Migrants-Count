Data and methods. Death records are from the IOM Missing Migrants Project (2014–2026), filtered to the four sea corridors that lead to Europe — Central, Western and Eastern Mediterranean, plus the Western Africa / Atlantic route to the Canary Islands. A small number of recorded incident coordinates fall far from the named country of incident (sign-flipped longitudes, swapped lat/lon, or shared placeholder coordinates); those are auto-corrected where the fix is unambiguous and dropped otherwise. Basemap from CartoDB · OpenStreetMap contributors, used under ODbL. All code, data and replication package are at github.com/data-journalism-26/data-bit-1-giorgio.
