Data and methods. Death records are from the IOM Missing Migrants Project (2014–2026, filtered to corridors leading to Europe via IOM's Route field) and from UNITED for Intercultural Action's List of Refugee Deaths (1993–2026, Europe-bound by NGO mandate). Both datasets often record one row per individual death rather than per event; rows that share a coordinate and date are aggregated into a single incident, summing dead/missing — so each circle on the map corresponds to one event, not to one row. UNITED's crossing_countries field (semicolon-joined ISO-2 codes) is mapped to IOM's Route labels so the route filter shares one vocabulary across the two sources; ambiguous Spain-Africa entries are split between Western Mediterranean and the Atlantic-to-Canaries route by latitude. Records that geocode outside the Europe-bound envelope (lon −25° to 55°, lat 10° to 75°) are excluded for clarity. Basemap from CartoDB · OpenStreetMap contributors, used under ODbL. All code, data and replication package are at github.com/data-journalism-26/data-bit-1-giorgio.
